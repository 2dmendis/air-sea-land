NVOCC at Air Sea Land

Air Sea Land Shipping is a FMC licensed and bonded non-vessel operating common carrier (NVOCC) headquartered in New York City, providing first class shipping services with a personal touch. We offer customized end-to-end ocean, air, and ground freight services, and are dedicated to customer satisfaction and service excellence. Air Sea Land is your single-source solution for your unique shipping needs.

What We Offer


Air Freight

  • Global export and import air freight services

  • Full Services Include:
    • Cargo Pickup
    • Warehousing
    • Labeling
    • Documentation
    • Customs Clearance at destination

  • Contracts with major airline carriers with access to special discounted rates

  • Fast transport with routes

Ocean Freight

  • Global export and import freight services

  • Door-to-Door, Door-to-Port, Port-to-Door, or Port-to-Port basis

  • Full container load (FCL), Less than container load (LCL), RoRo (Automobile only), Project and heavy lift

  • Extensive network of port agents worldwide for efficient, reliable, and cost-effective inland and destination door deliveries

  • Handling of all documentation, insurance, customs clearance, and filing requirements

Ground Freight

  • Domestic ground transportation throughout the United States

  • Movement of oversized or heavyweight cargo

  • Warehousing facilities

  • Full-Service Door-to-Door residential or office moves

  • Scheduling and handling of any necessary building paperwork

  • High caliber, experienced packing crew

  • Fully bonded and insured
